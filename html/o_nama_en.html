Who is Bogdan?

Legends of a cat bearing this name origin from ancient Egypt - food-related rituals were held in her name, an entire dynasty of royal cooks was named after her, and in Tutankhamun's tomb, a Yugioh card depicting her was found. Therefore it's no coincidence that a certain cat owned by M.P. from Valjevo, which has shown immense passion towards the art of cooking (mostly consuming its products), is named - Bogdan.

Our goal is to attract new and experienced cooks, and to give them a place where they can work on themselves and help others. This is why it wasn't difficult to, with the permission of his owner (we didn't ask him, but surely he'd allow it), choose Bogdan as our website's mascot, hoping that his glorious presence and enthusiasm will endear as many users as possible.

Why use Bogdan's Cookbook?

Nikola, 40, one of our happy users

If you want to improve your culinary skills - you've come to the right place. We offer you numerous recipes of different kinds and difficulty levels, curated by our users. And when you give them a shot, show us your attempts - or something entirely original - and you'll receive useful advice from more experienced cooking lovers. Our community is passionate, sincere, and won't hesitate to tell you that your creations don't even qualify as dog food (or cat food, which Bogdan can confirm).

If you're still unsure, maybe this will convince you: In a survey conducted on 29th April 2021, a whopping 92% of Bogdan's Cookbook users were more than satisfied with their cooking, and 86% of them consider themselves to be the best cooks they've heard of. Only our website can give you such (completely justified) confidence!
